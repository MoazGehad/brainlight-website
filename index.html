An Integrated Support System

AR-Based Games

Our AR approach uses live object detection, replacing traditional flashcards for a real-world learning experience. Engaging games to improve skills like memory and learning

AI Chatbot Assistant

Medically-verified, empathetic support for parents, powered by Gemini.

Ask a question to see an AI-generated response.

Emotion Recognition

AI-powered tools to help children identify and express emotions.

Personalized Reports

Clear, visual progress reports for parents to track development.

Adjust the sliders and click generate.

Cloud-Native & Scalable

A robust, cross-platform solution built to grow with families.

Grounded in Science

Our methodology is built upon established psychological theories like the "Triad of Impairiments" and the "Double Empathy Problem." We have validated our approach through consultations with doctors, therapists, and autism experts to ensure our solution is clinically relevant and ethically sound.
