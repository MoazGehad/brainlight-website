An Integrated AI & AR Ecosystem

Engage, learn, and grow — BrAIn Light connects children, parents, and doctors through play, empathy, and data-driven insight.

REAL-WORLD LEARNING

AR Object Detection

Our AR-powered learning replaces flashcards with real-time object detection. Children simply point the camera at real items — like an apple or a cup — and the app names them aloud in Arabic or English, turning everyday moments into lessons.

PLAY-BASED THERAPY

Skill-Building Games

Our bilingual games — Matching, Memory Cards, Puzzles, and Object Questions — improve attention, memory, and recognition skills. Each game adapts to the child’s ability and tracks measurable progress through stars and scores.

SOCIAL CONNECTION

Emotion Detection Game

Using facial expression recognition, children playfully identify emotions like happiness, sadness, or surprise. This builds empathy, improves nonverbal understanding, and supports emotional development.

24/7 GUIDANCE

AI Chatbot Assistant

Available anytime in Arabic and English, our chatbot offers verified advice and empathetic support for parents, using real progress data from the child’s activity to personalize guidance.

PROFESSIONAL CARE

Doctor’s Dashboard & Appointments

Doctors can access detailed progress dashboards and book or manage follow-up appointments directly through the app — simplifying continuous care and communication between families and specialists.

SMART INSIGHTS

Personalized Reports

Parents receive clear, visual progress reports summarizing performance, attention, and emotional stability. AI transforms daily scores into easy-to-read insights and suggestions.
